What is Magnum 4D?
Numbers drawn in the 4D lottery can be any sequence of four numbers between 0000 and 9999. Via various ploys and strategies, you win when your chosen digits coincide with the 4D numbers. Every lottery draw day, 23 sets of numbers are drawn and divided into five winning groups. Three of them become the first, second, and third prizes. Ten sets become special prizes and ten consolation prizes. You can bet on the lottery in two ways: big bet and small bet.
Big bets: The minimum bet amount is RM1. You stand to win if your numbers are in one of the five following groups: the first, second, and third prizes, special prizes, and consolation prizes. This gives you 23 winning chances, for a lower payout
Small bets: The minimum bet amount is also RM1. This has only three winning groups: the first, second, and third prizes. This gives you only 3 winning chances, for a higher payout.
Top Tips To Won Online Lotteries
1. Your lucky number or a randomly generated number? Some people put great faith in their personal lucky number. Others would rather trust random numbers generated by a machine. Which one works better? Believe it or not, it really doesn’t make the slightest difference. Most players soon conclude their lucky number has no special powers and quickly switch to the random number option.
2. Buying lots of lottery tickets. The more tickets you purchase, the more likely you are to win. Many people form syndicates and pool their money, buying several tickets and splitting the winnings between them. This method obviously boosts your chances of winning. Some might see sharing the winnings as a downside, but a share of something beats a share of nothing.
3. Discovering the best online lottery operators. Your odds will differ from operator to operator. Avoid the ones with the lowest odds. They’re simply not worth playing.
4. Picking the perfect permutation. Birthdays, anniversaries, and other memorable dates feature fairly highly as many people’s go-to lists of numbers. So it’s no surprise there’s a plethora of numbers from one to 31, but not many higher than 31. Therefore, choosing these numbers could be a good way to avoid sharing lucrative prizes with fellow players.
